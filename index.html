About me

Ruby on Rails / Front-end Developer, Certified ScrumMaster

My name is Konrad Jurkowski and I live in Warsaw, Poland. I'm a programmer specialized mainly in web applications, but I also have some experience creating mobile applications. For a day-to-day web development I prefer using Ruby on Rails, with technology stack consisting of Sass, CoffeeScript, Slim and... of course, Ruby.

As a developer, I always focus on delivering a tested and scalable product of very high standard. I use continuous integration systems and deploy the application to staging environments with production settings to test its real-life behavior.

When I don't write any code, I'm probably doing some gardening, wasting time watching yet another cool and popular TV Series or playing on PS4. 👻
